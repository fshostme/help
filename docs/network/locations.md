# Server Locations

Choose the server location closest to your players for the best performance and lowest latency.

::: tip Availability
Not all locations are available on both Free and Pro tiers. Check the [pricing page](https://fshost.me/pro/pricing) for your game to see location availability.
:::

## How to Choose a Location

Consider these factors when selecting your server location:

1. **Player Geography** - Where are most of your players located?
2. **Latency** - Choose the location closest to the majority of your players
3. **Time Zones** - Consider when your players are most active
4. **Availability** - Some locations may be Pro-only

::: tip Testing Latency
Before creating your server, [test ping to our locations](/network/ping-test) to find the best one for your players.
:::

## Changing Locations

::: warning
You cannot move servers between locations yourself.
:::

To move your server:
- Contact support via Discord or ticket (Pro)
- We'll process the request when available
- Limited to 2-3 moves per server

If you regularly need multiple regions, create separate servers for each location.

**Choose carefully during initial setup!**

## Location Availability

- **Free Servers**: Limited to select locations (varies by game)
- **Pro Servers**: Access to all available locations

Check the pricing page for your specific game to see current location availability and slots.
