# CS2 Demo Retention Change

**Effective Date**: 31st May 2026

From 31st May, we're reducing how long your CS2 demos are kept from 14 days to 7 days. After this date, any
demo older than 7 days will be automatically deleted. If you have older demos you'd like to keep, download them before 31st May.

## Why we're changing it
We're purely just optimising our storage based on usage patterns. CS2 is a large-size game to start with,
plus every demo and workshop map stored takes more and more NVMe SSD space. Depending on location, our costs
for dedicated servers change a lot, and the specs do too. Some providers limit storage per plan; we don't.
